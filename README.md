# Task 5: Superstore Sales Analysis

## Objective
Analyze the Superstore sales dataset to identify revenue trends, top-performing products/categories, and regional performance.

## Steps Performed
1. Loaded and explored the dataset (shape, info, describe).
2. Checked for missing values and removed duplicates.
3. Converted `Order Date` and `Ship Date` to datetime format.
4. Extracted `Year`, `Month`, and `Month_Year` for time-based analysis.
5. Plotted monthly revenue trend over time.
6. Analyzed sales by category and identified top 10 best-selling products.
7. Analyzed profit by category to compare with sales.
8. Compared sales and profit performance across regions.
9. Explored the relationship between discount and profit.
10. Summarized yearly sales and profit.
11. Saved the cleaned dataset as `superstore_cleaned.csv`.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib & Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Outcome
Identified key sales trends — top categories, best-selling products, regional performance, and the impact of discounts on profit margins, providing actionable insights for business decisions.
